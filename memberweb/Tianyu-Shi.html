Tianyu Shi

M.Eng.

Google Scholar Website

Tianyu received his bachelor degree in vehicle engineering from Beijing Institute of Technology. During his undergraduate study, he was a visiting student researcher at Berkeley Deep Drive, University of California Berkeley. Prior to joining McGill, he worked as research intern in L4 automated driving group at Momenta.ai. He also worked as research intern in based model group at Megvii Technology.

Research Interests

  • Intelligent robotics
  • Deep neural network
