Chengyuan Zhang

M.Eng.

Google Scholar Website

Chenyuan is the M.Eng. student with research interests in Automated Vehicles and Intelligent Transportation Systems. Specifically, he is willing to contribute his mathematical and physical knowledge to the application of Statistical Learning (especially probabilistic graphical model) in micro/macro driving behavior analysis, traffic scenarios patterns recognition and scene understanding, which would help with making better decisions of intelligent agents and higher efficiency of urban transportation systems.

Research Interests

  • Statistical learning
  • Micro/macro driving behavior analysis
