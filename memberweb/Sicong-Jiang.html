Sicong Jiang

Ph.D. candidate

Github Google Scholar Website

Sicong joined the Smart Transport Group at McGill University in fall, 2021. He received the B.S. degree in Automation from Northeastern University, Shenyang, China and the M.S. degree in Electrical & Computer Engineering from Georgia Institute of Technology, GA, USA. His research focused on applications of deep reinforcement learning as well as other machine learning technologies in intelligent transportation and urban computing.

Research Interests

  • Machine learning
  • Urban computing
